Frequently asked questions

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button at the top of the page and follow the prompts to enter your information.

How do I reset my master password?

To reset your master password, you can go to profile page and change your password there.

What if I forget my master password?

Then contact customer support for help. You would get link on your authorised email to change your password.

How do I contact customer support?

To contact customer support, call us at 1800-1200-6563 or email us at csupportsafepass@gmail.com

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account, go to profile page and click the option of deleting the account.
